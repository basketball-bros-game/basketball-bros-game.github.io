What is Sand Tetris?

Sand Tetris (or Sandtrix) blends the timeless block-stacking fun of Tetris with gravity-based sand physics. Instead of rigid blocks, your pieces behave like sand, creating unpredictable and exciting challenges. The goal remains the same: clear lines to score points and survive as long as possible!

How to Play Sand Tetris

One of the best things about Sand Tetris is its simplicity. All you need is your keyboard (or mouse for some versions), and you're ready to go. Here's how to play:

Basic Controls:

  • Arrow Left/Right – Move the sand block sideways
  • Arrow Down – Accelerate the block's fall
  • Arrow Up or Spacebar – Rotate the block
  • P – Pause the game

Tips to Master Sand Tetris

  1. Plan Ahead: Unlike classic Tetris, sand physics can be unpredictable. Think a step ahead to avoid unstable piles.
  2. Use Rotation Wisely: Rotating sand blocks can help them settle into gaps.
  3. Speed is Key: As levels progress, blocks fall faster. Stay sharp!
  4. Clear Multiple Lines: Aim for Tetrises (4-line clears) for maximum points.
